How to Play Snakes and Ladders

Objective:

Be the first player to reach square 100!

Game Rules:

  • Roll the dice to move your piece
  • Land on a ladder bottom to climb up
  • Land on a snake head to slide down
  • Must roll exact number to reach 100
  • If you roll over 100, bounce back
  • If you get a three 6s, your score will be 0 and the turn will go to the next player

Controls:

  • Click "Roll Dice" to play
  • Follow the numbered squares 1-100
  • Take turns with other players
